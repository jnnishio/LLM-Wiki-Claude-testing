## Concepts

### Active Projects
- [[project-nova]] — VRC-ATM-2201; flagship atmospheric monitoring; 14 Kryon-7 nodes; ends Jun 2024
- [[project-helios]] — VRC-SOL-1887; 10-year solar irradiance baseline; 4 Peltex-200 stations
- [[project-aether]] — VRC-ALT-4450; high-altitude sampling to 32 km; balloon + Atlas-X
- [[project-sable]] — VRC-OPT-5512; polar night optical survey; Atlas North + Valdyne
- [[project-triton]] — VRC-SUB-3304; under-ice bathymetric mapping; Atlas-II subsurface vehicle
- [[project-nexus]] — VRC-FUS-6671; 6-sensor fusion platform; 22.1M VRC-credits; largest VRC program ever

### Sensors and Instruments
- [[kryon-7]] — SYS-KRY7-001; primary multi-spectral atmospheric sensor; 4 bands; used in NOVA, HELIOS, AETHER
- [[kryon-7x]] — SYS-KRY7X-002; advanced sensor; 6 bands; 15× better noise floor; used in SABLE, AETHER (Atlas-X), NEXUS
- [[peltex-200]] — SYS-PEL200-R02 (CURRENT); broadband radiometric sensor; CRITICAL: K_cal = 0.00391 (old 0.00447 was wrong)
- [[valdyne-spectral-array]] — SYS-VAL-004; optical spectrograph 300–900 nm; key LVF spacing 14.77 mm; SABLE, AETHER, NEXUS

- [[duskline-protocol]] — PROT-DSK-003; data quality and anomaly management; 72-hour observation window
