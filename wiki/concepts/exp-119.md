# Experiment EXP-119

**Experiment Code:** EXP-119-2022  
**Lead Scientist:** [[sable-yurin]]  
**Facility:** [[oswick-station]] (deployment) + [[halvern-testing-ground]] (analytical work)  
**Period:** 15 January 2022 – 14 July 2023  
**Status:** Completed; final report issued (VRC-TR-EXP119-2023)

## Overview

Investigated long-term corrosion of sensor housing materials in cryospheric sub-ice water bodies at VRC northern sites. Motivated by observed accelerated surface degradation on [[kryon-7]] and [[peltex-200]] housings from Oswick Station under-ice sampling. Water chemistry data from [[project-triton]] CTD dives fed into corrosion rate models.

Both EXP-119 and [[project-triton]] are led by [[sable-yurin]]. Data sharing governed by VRC-DSA-2022-TRITON-119 (executed 15 Jan 2022).

## Materials Tested (8 coupons, 80 m depth, 18 months)

| Coupon | Material | Coating | Corrosion Rate (μm/year) |
|--------|----------|---------|-------------------------|
| C-01 | Marine-grade anodised Al 6061-T6 | Standard VRC anodise | **8.7** (highest) |
| C-02 | Marine-grade anodised Al 6061-T6 | VRC enhanced anodise v2 | 3.4 |
| C-03 | 316L stainless steel | None | 0.3 |
| C-04 | 316L stainless steel | PVD nitride coating | 0.1 |
| C-05 | PEEK polymer (Victrex 450G) | None | **0.0** (nil) |
| C-06 | Fibre-reinforced epoxy (VRC-COMP-01) | Gel coat | 1.2 |
| C-07 | Titanium alloy (Ti-6Al-4V) | None | <0.1 |
| C-08 | Carbon-aramid composite (VRC-CA-02) | None | 0.2 |

Classified as moderately aggressive for aluminium, mildly aggressive for stainless steel.

## Critical Finding: Standard Housing Material

The standard VRC anodised aluminium alloy (C-01) — used in **both [[kryon-7]] and [[peltex-200]] housings** — showed the highest corrosion rate: **8.7 μm/year**. Based on pit growth rate of 0.182 mm/year, estimated housing service life at Oswick-type sites: **~14.3 years** before housing wall thickness falls below 0.5 mm safety margin (starting from 3.1 mm). ^[raw/documents/document_21.md]

The enhanced anodise v2 (C-02) reduced corrosion rate to 3.4 μm/year (~2.6× improvement).

## Recommendations

1. **New deployments at Oswick and Halvern under-ice sites:** Specify enhanced anodise v2 coating (C-02 equivalent) for all aluminium-housed instruments.
2. **Existing Kryon-7 and Peltex-200 units at OSW-IS-03:** Inspect for housing wall thickness every 3 years.
