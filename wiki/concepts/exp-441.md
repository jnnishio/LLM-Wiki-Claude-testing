# Experiment EXP-441

**Experiment Code:** EXP-441-2023  
**Lead Scientist:** Dr. Noel Hartbury (Team Velanthor)  
**Facility:** [[halvern-testing-ground]] (test corridor HLV-CORRIDOR-01)  
**Period:** 03 March 2023 – 28 November 2023  
**Status:** Completed  
**Correction report:** VRC-TR-EXP441-CORR-2024 (issued 15 February 2024)

## Overview

Investigated thermal boundary layers around an autonomous aerial platform (Atlas-X) operating at low altitude over cold terrain, and quantified measurement bias this introduces on [[kryon-7]] and [[peltex-200]] sensors. Purpose: develop correction algorithms for [[project-nova]] and [[project-helios]] airborne data.

## Platform Configuration

- Platform: Atlas-X unit SYS-ATL-X002-S01 (first production unit); operated under Rev 4.1 firmware
- Payload (8.31 kg total — within both Rev 3.x 8.4 kg and Rev 4.x 9.1 kg limits):

| Item | Mass |
|------|------|
| [[kryon-7]] | 2.14 kg |
| [[peltex-200]] | 3.70 kg |
| Thermal imaging array (VRC-TIR-03) | 1.85 kg |
| Custom data logger | 0.62 kg |

## Reference System

Fixed mast HLV-MAST-02 (28 m) at [[halvern-testing-ground]] with:
- 12× calibrated PRT-100 (±0.05°C) for temperature reference profiles
- 2× [[kryon-7]] (cross-validation)
- 1× [[peltex-200]] (cross-validation)

Ambient temperature range during Phase C: −12°C to +4°C.

## Critical Baseline Temperature

**T_baseline = −47.3°C**

Measured at HLV-MAST-02, 2 m AGL, 27–28 February 2023, 06:00–08:00 local time. The most extreme ambient temperature recorded at Halvern in 22 years. Chosen deliberately for its extremity (to characterise platform behaviour under the coldest expected NOVA Zone B winter conditions). All EXP-441 bias correction coefficients normalised to this baseline. ^[raw/documents/document_15.md]

## Key Findings

### Thermal Boundary Layer (at 70 km/h cruise)

| Region | Boundary Layer Thickness |
|--------|------------------------|
| Forward fuselage | 3.1 mm (laminar) |
| Aft fuselage | 12.4 mm (turbulent) |
| Near payload bay | 18.7 mm (cavity-induced recirculation) |

### Sensor Bias

| Sensor | Bias at 70 km/h | Bias at 120 km/h | Temp Dependence |
|--------|----------------|-----------------|----------------|
| Kryon-7 Band 1 | +0.31°C | +0.74°C | 0.012°C/°C ambient |
| Kryon-7 Band 4 | +0.08°C | +0.19°C | Negligible |
| Peltex-200 | −0.4 W/m² | −1.1 W/m² | 0.03 W/m²/°C |

Peltex-200 shows a **negative** bias (underestimate) due to partial shading of solar aperture by heated exhaust air from motor cooling vents.
