# Oswick Station

**Facility Code:** FAC-OSW-001  
**ICAO:** XOSW  
**Location:** 74°23'19.4" N, 20°07'44.8" E (312 m AMSL)  
**Established:** 1997  
**Station Commander:** Lt. Cmd. [[bellamy-vorsch]] (concurrent with [[halvern-testing-ground]] since March 2023)  
**Manual Version:** 5.1 (issued 15 February 2023)

## Overview

VRC's primary northern polar research station. Year-round staffed. Serves as the anchor for [[project-nova]] Zone A sensor nodes, the Clearwater Protocol (see [[clearwater-protocol]]) northern operations, and subsurface survey access for [[project-triton]].

Completed 365 days of continuous operation in 2023 without an unplanned outage — first such full year in station history. ^[raw/documents/document_25.md]

## Location and Access

- **Primary access:** Fixed-wing to XOSW airstrip (1,800 m unpaved, cleared Oct–Apr)
- **Secondary access:** Tracked overland convoy via Route OSW-GROUND-02 (June–August only)
- Nearest settlement: Halvdal (148 km SE)
- Travel requests required 14 days in advance via VRC Logistics Portal

## Safety Protocols

- Cold-weather protocol OSW-COLD-01: required below −35°C (min 2-person teams, hourly radio check-in, 45-min max exposure, PPE VRC-PPE-POLAR-02)
- Mast work (OSW-T01) requires VRC-CERT-HEIGHT-03; prohibited above 14 m/s wind
- Emergency muster: OSW-EMP-01 (north face of OSW-B01)
- Emergency: ext. 7-700
