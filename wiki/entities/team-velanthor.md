# Team Velanthor

**Team Code:** TM-VEL-001  
**Team Lead:** [[mara-fenwick]]  
**Chartered:** 01 September 2019 (Charter Revision 4 as of 20 August 2023)  
**Size:** 12 active members (as of Aug 2023)  
**Primary base:** [[halvern-testing-ground]] (HLV-B01 offices and VDPC)  
**Secondary base:** [[oswick-station]] (Zone A field operations)

## Mission

Team Velanthor is one of VRC's two primary research teams, specialising in atmospheric science, field instrumentation, and high-latitude environmental monitoring. Formed from the former VRC Atmospheric Sciences Division. Reports to [[tarquin-selvidge]].
