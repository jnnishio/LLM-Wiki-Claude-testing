# Team Crestfield

**Team Code:** TM-CRE-002  
**Team Lead:** [[renna-holt]]  
**Chartered:** 15 March 2018 (Charter Revision 6 as of 01 November 2023)  
**Size:** 8 active members (as of Nov 2023)  
**Primary base:** [[halvern-testing-ground]] (HLV-B03, ISO 7 calibration laboratory)  
**Secondary:** Workshop bay HLV-B02-EAST; periodic field visits to [[oswick-station]]

## Mission

VRC's instrumentation and sensor engineering team. Holds **technical authority** over all VRC scientific instruments. Responsible for design, build, calibration, and lifecycle maintenance of all VRC sensors. Reports to [[tarquin-selvidge]].
